Formula Resolvente

Solve quadratic equations quickly and easily with this simple calculator!

  1. Enter the coefficients a, b, and c of the quadratic equation ax2 + bx + c = 0.
  2. Click on the "Calcular" button.
  3. The solutions for x will be displayed.
  4. If the discriminant b2 - 4ac is negative, indicating no real solutions, the message "No real solutions" will be displayed.
